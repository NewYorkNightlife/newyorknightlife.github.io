# Editorial Truth + Audit Policy (Permanent)

## Non-negotiable truth rule
- Never publish fabricated information.
- Never publish fake events, specials, discounts, listings, or news.
- If uncertain, do not publish.

## Source requirement
- Use inline citations in post body + clickable source links section at bottom.
- For time-sensitive or commercial claims (specials, discounts, lineups, listings):
  - minimum **3 independent sources** before publish.

## Weekly publishing cadence
- Friday posts publish at **2:00 PM America/New_York**.
- Backfill archive also uses Friday 2:00 PM ET timestamps.
