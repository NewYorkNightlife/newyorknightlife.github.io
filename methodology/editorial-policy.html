Editorial Policy

We publish NYC nightlife recommendations with a usefulness-first standard. Our priority is helping readers decide faster and avoid low-quality options.

How we choose what to cover

  • User intent (what people are actually trying to decide tonight/weekend).
  • Neighborhood relevance (practical planning by area, not generic city lists).
  • Decision utility (pricing context, timing windows, vibe fit, alternatives).

How we evaluate quality

  • Door/logistics reality (lines, entry friction, reservation constraints).
  • Price-to-experience value.
  • Consistency over hype.
  • Safety and accessibility considerations where relevant.

Updates and corrections

  • We update pages when hours, policies, pricing, or availability materially change.
  • If an item is outdated or incorrect, we revise and timestamp updates.

Commercial relationships

Some links are affiliate links. If you book through them, we may earn a commission at no extra cost to you. Commercial relationships do not override quality standards.
