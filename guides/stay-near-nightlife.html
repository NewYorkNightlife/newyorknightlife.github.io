Pro Tips for Hotel Selection

  • Subway Access: Stay near a major subway line (A, C, L, 6 lines) for safe late-night returns
  • 24-Hour Service: Hotels with 24-hour front desk are better for late-night returns
  • Free Cancellation: Nightlife plans change. Book with free cancellation
  • Read Nightlife Reviews: Look for mention of "walking distance to bars" in reviews
  • Weekend Rates: Friday-Saturday are 30-50% more expensive. Book weekdays if possible
  • Airbnb Option: Shared apartments in nightlife neighborhoods are often cheaper. Use Airbnb

Getting Home Safely After Nightlife

  • Subway: 24-hour service on major lines (A, C, F, M, N, Q, R, W)
  • Taxi/Uber: $15-30 depending on distance. Always use official service
  • Car Service: Safer option after 2am. $20-40 from downtown to hotel
  • Walk in Groups: Never walk alone. Stay with friends until you reach your hotel
  • Stay Aware: NYC is safe but stay alert, especially late night
