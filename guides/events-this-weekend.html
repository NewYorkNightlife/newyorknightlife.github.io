Pro Tips for Event Planning

  • Plan Ahead: Book tickets 1-2 days early. Popular events sell out.
  • Check Dress Code: Upscale clubs require smart casual. Dive bars are casual.
  • Arrive Early: Get to venues before 11pm for easier entry and cheaper cover.
  • Use Skip-the-Line: Many platforms (GetYourGuide) offer skip-the-line options.
  • Get Discounts: Group packages usually offer better pricing.
  • Stay Safe: Stick with your group. Use official transport home (subway, taxi, car service).
