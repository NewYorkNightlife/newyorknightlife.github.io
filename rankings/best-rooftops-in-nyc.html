Rooftop nights fail when people treat them like random walk-ins. Win rate goes up when you pick your tier, lock timing windows, and sequence nearby backups.

Execution tips

  • Arrive in the 6:30–8:30 PM window when possible.

  • Don’t cross-town pivot unless upside is clear.
