Weekend Overview

March 6 is a precision weekend, not a hype weekend. You can outperform with fewer moves and tighter transfers. Groups that over-index on optionality usually lose prime hours. Groups that pre-define their objective (music-first, social-first, low-cost, premium) get cleaner outcomes.

What Changed This Week

Compared with month-end February, this week shifts toward show-led anchors. Ticketed rooms pull stronger first-wave demand, while social bar routes peak slightly later. That changes timing: start with certainty, then pivot local.

Night Route Strategies
