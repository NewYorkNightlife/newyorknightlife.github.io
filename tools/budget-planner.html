Budget Breakdown

Here's how NYC nightlife spending typically breaks down:

Item Cost Notes
Dive Bar Drink $5-8 Beer, whiskey, basic cocktails
Mid-Range Cocktail $12-16 Nice bars, quality ingredients
Premium Cocktail $16-22 Rooftop bars, upscale lounges
Club Cover Charge $0-50 Varies by venue and day
Happy Hour Drink $5-7 5-7pm daily at most bars

Pro Budget Tips

  • Happy Hour: Drink $5-7 cocktails from 5-7pm. Best value in NYC.
  • Bar Crawls: Organized crawls include drink specials at each venue.
  • Group Discounts: Groups of 8+ get better pricing on clubs and tours.
  • Wednesday Nights: Cheaper night. Many venues run specials.
  • Beer Over Cocktails: Save money by ordering beer instead of cocktails.
  • Free Events: Check Events page for free nightlife activities.
